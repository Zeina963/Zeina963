# Zeinab Abu Romman

### Water Resources Engineer | PhD | P.Eng. | GISP | Data Scientist

I work at the intersection of water systems, climate science, geospatial analysis, and data.

My focus is understanding how hydrologic and groundwater systems respond to:
- climate variability  
- allocation pressure  
- long-term sustainability challenges  

and translating that into structured, defensible analysis for real-world decision-making.

---

### Core Areas

- Water allocation modelling and WRMM analysis  
- Surface water and groundwater modelling  
- Climate-informed water availability assessment  
- Basin-scale water balance analysis  
- GIS and remote sensing  
- Data automation and decision-support systems  

---

### Tools and Systems

WRMM | MODFLOW (GMS) | HEC-HMS | HEC-RAS | ArcGIS Pro  
Python | R | SQL | ArcPy | Power BI | Excel automation  

---

### Selected Work

**SSRB Water Allocation Analysis**  
WRMM-based supply-demand assessment, diversion and deficit analysis, and drought response tools supporting basin-scale water management in Alberta.

**Groundwater Recharge Modelling**  
3D MODFLOW model assessing climate change impacts under RCP 4.5 and RCP 8.5, identifying significant long-term recharge decline.

**Hydrologic Automation and Dashboards**  
Python, R, and GIS workflows for automated data processing, QA/QC, visualization, and regulatory reporting.
