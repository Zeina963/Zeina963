### 👩‍💼 About Me

Water Resources Engineer with Government of Alberta experience in water allocation modelling, basin-scale hydrologic assessment, drought response analysis, and regulatory decision support under Alberta’s Water Act.

My work focuses on understanding climate change impacts on water availability and system behaviour through integrated surface water and groundwater modelling.

I specialize in combining:
- Hydrologic modelling
- Groundwater modelling
- GIS and remote sensing
- Data science and automation

to support practical, real-world water management challenges.

I work extensively with large environmental datasets to evaluate:
- Precipitation
- Evapotranspiration
- Recharge
- Runoff
- Basin-scale water balance

using tools such as HEC-HMS, MODFLOW, WRMM, GIS, and Python-based workflows.
