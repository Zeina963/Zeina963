## Engineering water systems under climate uncertainty

I work at the intersection of water systems, climate science, geospatial analysis, and data.

My focus is understanding how hydrologic and groundwater systems respond to climate variability, allocation pressure, and long-term sustainability challenges, then translating that into practical decision-support tools.

### Current Focus

- Water allocation modelling and WRMM analysis
- Surface water and groundwater modelling
- Climate-informed water availability assessment
- Basin-scale water balance analysis
- GIS, remote sensing, and spatial analytics
- Data automation, QA/QC, and dashboards
